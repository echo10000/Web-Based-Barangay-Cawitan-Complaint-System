Terms

Guidelines for using the Barangay Cawitan Complaint Management System.

Back

Proper Use

The system is intended for legitimate barangay complaint submissions, case tracking, staff processing, administrative monitoring, and official reporting.

Accurate Information

Users must provide truthful and complete information. False, misleading, abusive, or unrelated submissions may be rejected, closed, or referred for appropriate action.

Account Responsibility

Each user is responsible for keeping their account secure. Do not share passwords or use another person's account without authorization.

Complaint Handling

Complaint status, assignment, remarks, notices, hearing details, fees, and resolution records may be updated by authorized staff or admin users as the case progresses.

System Availability

The system may be unavailable during maintenance, technical issues, or connectivity interruptions. Urgent matters should be raised directly with the barangay office.
