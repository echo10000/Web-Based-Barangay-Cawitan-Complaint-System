About the System

A digital front desk for the barangay.

The Barangay Cawitan Complaint Management System is an official online platform designed to make it easier for residents to file complaints and for barangay staff to respond to them efficiently.

No more long waits or unclear processes — everything is tracked, time-stamped, and visible to the right people.

Residents

Register, submit complaints, upload evidence, and track every status update.

Barangay Staff

Review assigned complaints, post updates, and mark resolutions with remarks.

Administrators

Oversee all complaints, manage staff accounts, assign cases, and generate reports.

{# ── HOW IT WORKS ───────────────────────────────── #}

The Process

How it works

Filing and resolving a complaint is a straightforward four-step process.

1

Register & Sign In

Create a free resident account using your name and contact details.

2

Submit a Complaint

Fill in the complaint form, choose a category, describe the issue, and attach evidence.

3

Staff Reviews

Barangay staff is assigned and begins reviewing your complaint with posted updates.

4

Get Resolved

You receive a notification when your complaint is resolved. Track it all the way through.
