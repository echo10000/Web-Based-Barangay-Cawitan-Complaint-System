Privacy Policy

How Barangay Cawitan handles complaint records and account information.

Back

Information Collected

The system stores account details, contact information, complaint descriptions, uploaded evidence, respondent details, status updates, staff remarks, and feedback submitted after resolution.

Purpose of Use

Information is used to receive complaints, verify resident accounts, assign staff, monitor case progress, notify involved users, generate reports, and maintain official barangay complaint records.

Access Control

Residents can view their own complaints. Staff can view complaints assigned to them or available for handling. Admin users can manage records, accounts, categories, reports, and system activity.

Record Protection

Users should keep their login credentials private. Admin and staff accounts must only access records needed for official complaint handling and barangay operations.

Updates and Corrections

Residents may update their profile details from their account page. For complaint record corrections, contact the barangay office or authorized staff.
