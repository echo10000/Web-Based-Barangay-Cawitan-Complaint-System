Privacy Policy

How Barangay Cawitan handles complaint records and account information.

Back

Information Collected

The system stores account details, contact information, complaint descriptions, uploaded evidence, respondent details, status updates, staff remarks, and feedback submitted after resolution.

Purpose of Use

Information is used only for official barangay complaint handling, resident verification, case notices, service referrals, monitoring, legally required records, and account security. Political, campaign, partisan, vote-buying, or voter-targeting use is prohibited.

Access Control

Residents can view their own complaints. Staff can view complaints assigned to them or available for handling. Resident directory access requires an official purpose, contact details are masked in list views, and full resident profile access is audit logged. Bulk report exports require a written reason and independent approval before download.

Record Protection

Users should keep their login credentials private. Admin and staff accounts must only access records needed for official complaint handling and barangay operations. System audit records and export requests are retained as official accountability records and are not editable through the admin interface.

Updates and Corrections

Residents may update their profile details from their account page. For complaint record corrections, contact the barangay office or authorized staff.
