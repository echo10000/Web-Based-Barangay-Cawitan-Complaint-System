Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for the most common resident questions.

How do I file a complaint?

Click Submit Complaint, fill in the details, add the incident location and date, then submit the form for barangay review.

What happens after I submit?

Your complaint is marked Pending Review. Barangay staff will review it, update the status, and send notifications through your account.

How long does processing take?

The target response time depends on priority. Urgent concerns are handled faster, while normal complaints are typically reviewed within the listed category target.

Can I update my complaint?

You can check your complaint record from My Complaints. If more information is needed, contact the office or wait for staff instructions.
